Preventing Social media money laundering

Preventing Social media money laundering through social media entails integrating modules for Admin, User, Uploader, and Hospitals. Admin oversees compliance, User authentication prevents fraud, and Uploaders facilitate document verification, ensuring secure transactions and mitigating risks.

Evaluation of birds affected by mobile Tower

This project aims to assess the impact of mobile towers on birds by collecting a dataset of 100,000 observations. Utilizing logistic regression, the study seeks to predict the likelihood of birds being affected by mobile tower presence, facilitating insights into potential environmental consequences.
